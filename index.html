? HOW THIS TOOL WORKS
Step 1 – Compute Report Indicator

For each facility-period row: report = 1 if the SUM of your selected indicator columns > 0, else report = 0. You choose which columns define "reported".

Step 2 – Classify HFs With / Without Reports

Facilities that never have report = 1 in any period go to the no-reports output. The rest are analysed further.

Step 3 – Determine Active Status (Choose a Method)

Method 1 – Permanent: once a facility first reports, it is Active for all subsequent periods.
Method 2 – First-to-Last: Active only between its first and last reporting period.
Method 3 – Dynamic: Active immediately after reporting; becomes Inactive after N consecutive non-reporting periods; reactivates when it reports again.

Step 4 – Calculate Reporting Rate

RR = (Σ report where active=1) / (Σ active=1) × 100 — aggregated by location and time period. Also produces a simple all-HF rate without active-status filtering.
